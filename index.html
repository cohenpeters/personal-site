ABOUT COOLAS

This past summer me and two of my closest friends wanted to showcase what a summer in the most beautiful province in Canada looks like. We decided that a doc showcasing some of our favourite spots in beautiful British Columbia would not only do the trick but also give a glimpse into our sick lives. This project gave us an outlet to send it whenever we had a chance cause it was for the video. Hopefully the journey doesn’t end here and we can keep our lives exciting by keeping Coolas going.

F*CKING DO IT

Coolas started like any other project, I wanted to do something cool and had an idea of how to do it. With this said, 90% of my creative projects crash before they can even take off. Even this video wasn’t going to happen if it wasn’t for my friends doing sick shit with me. Now, this is one of my favourite stories I’ve ever captured. Whether is be photography, videography, or any other medium you can think of, DO IT. At the end of the day, I way rather regret doing something than regret not starting it in the first place. - Coolas Out

BIO

Cohen Peters is a photographer and filmmaker who discovered his love for capturing moments at the age of 15 when he bought his first camera. Starting with film photography, Cohen found storytelling through photos and videos. His keen interest in business and design inspires him to explore creative hybrids between the two fields. Currently, Cohen is pursuing a degree in Global Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) at the University of Waterloo, blending his design skills with a strong foundation in business and technology.
